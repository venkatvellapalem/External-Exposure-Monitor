Decoupled Attack Surface Architecture

EASM operates as an isolated threat monitoring authority:

  • Zero Target Traffic Bloat: Queries passive intelligence caches (Censys v3 & Shodan) without firing aggressive probes.
  • Surgical Verification: Lightweight 2.5-second Python TCP handshakes confirm real-time open ports.
  • Stateful Reconciliation: Tracks exposures and marks ports as closed automatically as risks are remediated.
